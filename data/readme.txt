Examples of use provided in: https://github.com/firefly-cpp/osm-graph

Contains: 
	- MongoDB json dump of individual road node locations (pathways.json),
	- Neo4j database dump of intersections and roads between them (slovenia-graph-neo4j.dump).

Based on: 
	- A. Rajšp, M. Heričko, and F. Jr. Iztok, "Preprocessing of roads in OpenStreetMap based geographic data on a property graph," 
in Proceedings of the Central European Conference on Information and Intelligent Systems, 2921, pp. 193–199. Accessed: Jun. 27, 2022. [Online]. 
Available: http://archive.ceciis.foi.hr/app/public/conferences/2021/Proceedings/IS/IS3.pdf

Data available at: https://doi.org/10.17632/zkbfvsjr5f.1
